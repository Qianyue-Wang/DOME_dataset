1. Lily wakes up one day to find that she has turned into a cat and her family does not know who she is.
2. Lily meets Max, another person who has also been turned into a cat, and the two form an instant bond.
3. Lily and Max work with another mysterious cat and an odd scientist to try to figure out what caused them to change and how to change back.
4. Lily and Max's families slowly begin to accept their new form and the two live happily together.
