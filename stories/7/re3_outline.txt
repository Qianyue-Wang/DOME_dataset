1. Kate Adams, an 18-year-old girl, begins to lose her sight and slips into a depression.
2. Kate's parents, Rebecca and David, decide to move her across the country to get better medical care for her condition, they want Kate to continue living at home.
3. Kate meets a new friend, Sara Michaels, and gets a guide dog that Joe Silver teachers her how to use and starts to adjust to her new life.
4. Kate begins attending college and starts to regain her independence.
