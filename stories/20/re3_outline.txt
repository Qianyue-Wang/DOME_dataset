1. Gail, Joe, Rick, Susan, Aaron, Maya, Kevin, Jules, Ian and Noah vacation on the island of Lemnos in the Mediterranean Sea and discover sunken ruins while snorkeling.
2. The group explored the ruins and discovered a Leviathan which trapped them in a loop between the present world and a Mayan-like society from the past. Joe and Susan learn to rely on each other.
3. The group tried to break the loop and escape but were unsuccessful and ended up back in the present world where they started. Joe and Susan have growing closeness. Gail, Jules and Ian try to solve the mystery from the other side, but can't seem to find the trigger.
