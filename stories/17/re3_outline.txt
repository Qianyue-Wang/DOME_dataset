1. Jace Garner and Reid Cortland are scientists working on a cybernetic implant that could bring about immortality for the citizens of Arta.
2. The implant unexpectedly starts a war between the three planets and Jace and Reid must find a way to stop it before it destroys all of them.
3. Along the way, Jace and Reid will meet Samantha Harper, Ethan Danvers, Rachel Woods, Nadia Ahmed, Troy Lambert, Garrick Kagan, and Alexander Kane; all of whom will help them in their quest to stop the war.
