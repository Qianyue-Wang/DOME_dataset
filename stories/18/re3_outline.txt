1. Eric Matthews, a college student, hasn't been able to experience the feeling of someone wanting to go out with him until he catches the eye of Lisa Nguyen, a beautiful girl in his class.
2. Lisa reciprocates Eric's feelings and the two start dating, much to the jealousy of Brad Johnson, a popular jock who also likes Lisa.
3. Brad begins to spread rumors about Eric in an attempt to sabotage their relationship, but Lisa remains loyal to him.
4. Eric and Lisa's relationship continues to grow stronger, and eventually, Brad accepts that there's nothing he can do to come between them.
