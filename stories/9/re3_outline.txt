1. Jane Davidson becomes President of the United States.
2. On her first day in office, Jane Davidson learns that aliens secretly exist and that her Vice President, Samuel Powell, is the alien ambassador disguised as a human.
3. Jane Davidson must follow her first day in office with a night meeting with the alien leaders on their hidden interstellar base, relying only on the guidance of Samuel Powell.
4. During the meeting with Zendrru Kkorrlk, Jane Davidson learns that the aliens want to establish a permanent presence on Earth in the United States and that her decision could determine her country's role in the future of humanity.
