1. Kitty Pawsky, the world's greatest detective, agrees to take on a case in which a string of murders have been committed in a dog park.
2. Kitty begins her investigation by speaking to the various dogs in the park, but none of them seem to be very helpful.
3. Kitty then turns her attention to the cats in the park, and eventually discovers that Sergei Snipe, a Russian Blue cat, is behind the murders.
4. Kitty confronts Sergei and eventually solves the case, bringing the murderer to justice.
