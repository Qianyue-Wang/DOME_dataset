1. Jenna Riley, a 21-year-old woman, is attacked and left for dead.
2. Jenna wakes up in the hospital a week later to find that she is now a vampire. 
3. Jenna must learn to navigate her new life while also trying to find her attacker and bring them to justice.
4. Along the way, Jenna will make new friends and enemies, and discover the truth about her attacker.
